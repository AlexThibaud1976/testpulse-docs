## Teams / Slack notifications

In **Settings → Notifications**, configure up to **three incoming webhooks** (name + URL + Teams / Slack). Use **Test** to send a sample message. After generating a report, click **Notify** to push its KPIs to the selected channels — an Adaptive Card for Teams, Block Kit for Slack.

<Note>On-premises CORS: if the browser blocks the request, TestPulse shows the JSON payload so you can copy-paste it manually.</Note>

## Scheduled reports

In **Settings → Scheduled reports**, schedule a plan (optionally a saved view) at a **weekly / biweekly / monthly** frequency. When a report is due, a reminder banner appears **at startup** with a **Generate now** button (it loads the saved view and generates).

<Warning>There is no backend — **reminders only appear while TestPulse is open**.</Warning>
