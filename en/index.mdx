TestPulse turns your **Azure DevOps Test Plans** into professional, shareable test reports — and an embedded quality signal — **without ever sending your data anywhere**. It runs entirely in your browser, inside Azure DevOps.

<Note>
  **100% client-side · free · no tiers.** TestPulse reads your Test Plans and renders reports locally. It is **read-only**, with one opt-in exception: the **Coverage Builder**, which can create test plans/suites for you.
</Note>

## What you can do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Generate a report" icon="file-export">
    Export polished reports as PDF, HTML, Word, Excel or PowerPoint from any Test Plan.
  </Card>
  <Card title="See coverage & quality" icon="shield-check">
    Coverage depth, a Quality Gate grade (A–E) and a GO/NO-GO release verdict.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Read the signal on the work item" icon="list-check">
    A "Test coverage" tab on user stories: scope selector and an execution verdict.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track trends & collaborate" icon="chart-line">
    History, trends, Teams/Slack notifications and scheduled reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who it is for

- **Product Owners / Test Managers** — a report to share and a clear GO/NO-GO.
- **QA & developers** — coverage, depth, failures, traceability, exports.
- **ADO admins** — install on cloud or on-premises, control permissions.

## What TestPulse is **not**

- Not a test **runner** — it reports on results, it doesn't execute tests.
- Not a paid funnel — there is no Pro tier and no paywall.
- Not a data processor — nothing leaves your Azure DevOps tenant.
